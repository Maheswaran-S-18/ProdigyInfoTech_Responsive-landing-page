01 · Clear & bright

Sunny

Unbroken light, long shadows, warm air that sits still. High pressure holds the clouds away and the horizon stays sharp from dawn to dusk.

0–10%

02 · Low pressure, steady fall

Rainy

Moisture-heavy clouds thicken until they let go. The air cools, sound flattens, and the ground drinks faster than it can hold.

Sky cover
80–100%
Humidity
85%+
Feel
Cool, close

03 · Below freezing, falling slow

Snowy

Water crystallizes on the way down instead of falling as rain. Everything quiets under a layer that keeps building until the temperature turns.

Temp
≤ 0°C
Visibility
Reduced
Feel
Still, muffled

04 · Sudden pressure drop

Windy & Stormy

Warm and cold air collide fast. Wind gusts, branches bend, and the sky can flip from calm to violent in the time it takes to find shelter.

40–90 km/h
Pressure
Falling fast
Feel
Charged, loud
